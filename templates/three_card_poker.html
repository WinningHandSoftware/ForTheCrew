Three Card Poker - Tips & Guidelines

How to Play

  • Game Type: Three-card stud poker (played with a single 52-card deck, no jokers).
  • Betting Options: Players can bet Ante, Pair Plus, 6 Card Bonus, or all three.
  • Dealer Qualification: The dealer must have a Queen-high or better to qualify.
  • Winning Against Dealer: If the dealer does not qualify, Play bet pushes; Ante wins.
  • Pair Plus: Pays based on hand value (not against the dealer).
  • 6 Card Bonus: Combines player and dealer cards to form the best 5-card hand.

Best Strategy Tips

  • Play with Q-6-4 or Better: If your hand is lower, folding is the best strategy.
  • Avoid Side Bets Unless Chasing Big Payouts: Side bets like Pair Plus and 6 Card Bonus have a higher house edge.
  • Know the Payouts: Straight Flush (40:1) and Three of a Kind (30:1) are the highest-paying Pair Plus hands.
  • Progressive Side Bet: Consider betting $1 for a chance at the jackpot.

Payout Tables

Pair Plus Payouts

  • Pair - 1:1
  • Flush - 3:1
  • Straight - 6:1
  • Three of a Kind - 30:1
  • Straight Flush - 40:1

Ante Bonus Payouts

  • Straight - 1:1
  • Three of a Kind - 4:1
  • Straight Flush - 5:1

6 Card Bonus Payouts

  • 5-Card Royal Flush - 1,000:1
  • 5-Card Straight Flush - 200:1
  • Four of a Kind - 50:1
  • Full House - 25:1
  • Flush - 15:1
  • Straight - 10:1
  • Three of a Kind - 5:1

Dealer Guidelines & Tips

  • Dealer Must Announce: "No More Bets" before dealing.
  • Shuffle Machine Must Be Used: Hand shuffling is not allowed for Progressive payouts.
  • Handling Cards: Players must keep their cards above the table and in full view.
  • Bet Tokes: Players can place tip bets for dealers (except Progressive bets).
  • Pay & Take Order: Pay from right to left; take losing bets first.

Progressive Jackpot

  • Requires a **$1 Progressive wager** placed with Ante or Pair Plus.
  • Ace-King-Queen of Spades: Wins 100% of the Progressive Jackpot.
  • Ace-King-Queen of Hearts, Diamonds, or Clubs: Pays 500 for 1.
  • Envy Bonus available for other players if someone hits a jackpot.

Table Limits

  • Pair Plus: Min $5 - Max $300
  • Ante/Play: Min $5 - Max $300
  • 6 Card Bonus: Min $5 - Max $100
  • Progressive Side Bet: Fixed at $1

Game Protection & Security

  • Always use the **automatic shuffler** – no hand shuffling allowed.
  • Ensure **cards are fully visible to surveillance**.
  • Watch for **players switching cards** – all cards must remain on the table.
  • Surveillance **must verify Royal Flush payouts** before paying.

Tipping Rules

  • No Soliciting Tokes: Dealers may not ask for tips.
  • Allowed Bets: Players can place tips on all bets **except Progressive**.
  • Max Dealer Tip: 10% of table max (if betting table max).
  • Parlaying Tips is Not Allowed: Each winning tip must be collected.
