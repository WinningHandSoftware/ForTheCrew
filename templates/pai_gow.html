Pai Gow Poker - Tips & Guidelines

How to Play

  • Each player receives seven cards and must create a 5-card High Hand and 2-card Low Hand.
  • To win, both hands must beat the dealer’s hands.
  • Joker is wild for straights, flushes, and straight flushes; otherwise, it is an Ace.

Dealer Tips & Procedures

  • Use House Way to set the dealer’s hand.
  • Collect all losing bets before paying winners.
  • Calculate and collect the 5% commission on winnings.
  • Ensure proper shuffle and deck verification.

Game Protection

  • Surveillance must verify all large payouts.
  • Players must keep their hands visible.
  • **No player banking allowed**.
