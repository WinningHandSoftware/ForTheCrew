How to Access WindCreek Connect

Follow these steps to access and utilize WindCreek Connect:

  1. Call the Solutions Center
    • 7 days after orientation, call 251-446-4357 and inform the agent:
      • Your Name
      • Your Property: WindCreek WCI
      • Reason for call: To receive username and password for WindCreek Connect and request a Windows Account (for logging into WindCreek devices).
      • Provide: Ticket Request Number
  2. First Time Log In
    • Using the computers in Lucky's (TDR), type your given username and password.
    • You will be prompted to create a new password.
    • Once logged in, click WindCreek Connect and sign in.
  3. Download WindCreek Connect
    • Go to the Google Play Store or Apple App Store to download the WindCreek Connect app on your phone.
  4. Utilizing WindCreek Connect
    • Use the provided packet to help with WindCreek Connect navigation.
    • For any issues, please contact the Solutions Center.

Raise Eligibility Guidelines

Employees can increase their base pay by mastering specific games as outlined below:

  • Base Pay Requirement:
    • At least one Major Game: Blackjack, Craps, Roulette, or Baccarat
    • At least one Novelty Game: Pai Gow, UTH, War, Mississippi, Three Card, or High Card Flush
  • Additional Pay Incentives:
    • Major Games: +$0.50/hour each for adding Blackjack, Baccarat, or Roulette; +$1.00/hour for Craps
    • Novelty Games: +$0.25/hour for adding 3 Novelty Games, +$0.50/hour for mastering all Novelty Games

Speak to your manager if you'd like to begin training for additional games.
