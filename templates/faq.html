Raise Eligibility Guidelines

Increase your base pay by mastering specific games:

  • Base Pay Requirement:
    • One Major Game: Blackjack, Craps, Roulette, or Baccarat
    • One Novelty Game: Pai Gow, UTH, War, Mississippi, Three Card, or High Card Flush
  • Additional Pay Incentives:
    • Major Games: +$0.50/hour for Blackjack, Baccarat, Roulette; +$1.00/hour for Craps
    • Novelty Games: +$0.25/hour for 3 Novelty Games, +$0.50/hour for all Novelty Games

Speak to your manager if you'd like to begin training for additional games.
