Roulette - Tips & Guidelines

How to Play

  • Game Type: Roulette (played on Single, Double, or Triple Zero wheels).
  • Winning Condition: The ball lands in a numbered pocket that matches the bet.
  • Dealerâ€™s Role: Spins the ball, calls winning numbers, and handles payouts.
  • Players Must: Place bets before the dealer announces **"No More Bets"**.

Betting Options & Payouts

Inside Bets

  • Straight Up (Single Number): 35:1
  • Split (2 Numbers): 17:1
  • Street (3 Numbers): 11:1
  • Corner (4 Numbers): 8:1
  • Five-Number Bet (0, 00, 1, 2, 3 - Double Zero Wheels): 6:1
  • Six-Line (6 Numbers): 5:1

Outside Bets

  • Column (12 Numbers): 2:1
  • Dozen (1-12, 13-24, 25-36): 2:1
  • Even Money Bets (Red/Black, Odd/Even, High/Low): 1:1

Special Bets

  • Courtesy Line (Covers 0 & 00): 11:1
  • 2G$ Side Bet (Back-to-Back 0s): 150:1 (Triple Zero) / 350:1 (Double Zero)

Dealer Tips & Procedures

  • Announce **"No More Bets"** before the ball slows down.
  • Ensure **proper buy-ins and color-up chips for players**.
  • Follow **strict payout procedures** to avoid mistakes.
  • Always **verify large payouts with a supervisor**.

Game Protection & Security

  • Monitor for **past posting** (placing late bets after the ball lands).
  • Watch for **pinching** (removing a losing bet after the spin).
  • Be aware of **team play and distraction tactics**.
  • Surveillance must verify **suspicious or large payouts**.
