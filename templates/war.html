Casino War - Tips & Guidelines

How to Play

  • Game Type: Casino War (played with **6 decks**).
  • Winning Condition: Highest card wins.
  • Dealer Burns One Card: Before the first deal.
  • Tie Rule: Players can surrender (lose half their bet) or **go to War** (double their bet).

Betting Options & Payouts

  • **Winning Primary Bet:** Pays **1:1**.
  • **Winning War Bet:** Pays **1:1** (if War is won).
  • **War Tie Win:** Pays **2:1**.
  • **Tie Wager:** Pays **10:1** (if the round is a tie).

Dealer Tips & Procedures

  • Announce **"No More Bets"** before dealing.
  • **Burn three cards before War rounds**.
  • Compare **each player's card against the dealer** and settle bets.
  • **Players cannot touch their cards**.
  • **Winning bets must be sized into before payout**.

Game Protection & Security

  • Monitor for **late bets or unusual patterns**.
  • Surveillance **must verify all large payouts**.
  • Ensure all **bets follow table limits**.
  • All decisions should be **escalated to a supervisor if needed**.

Dealer Tokes (Tips)

  • **Soliciting tips is prohibited**.
  • Winning tips **must be placed in the toke box immediately**.
  • **Max dealer tip bet = 10% of table max**.
