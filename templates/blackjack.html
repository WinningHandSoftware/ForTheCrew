Blackjack - Tips & Guidelines

How to Play

  • Game Type: Blackjack (played with 1-8 decks).
  • Winning Condition: Get **closer to 21** than the dealer without busting.
  • Dealer Rules: Must hit on **16 or lower** and stand on **17 or higher**.
  • Blackjack (Ace + 10): Pays **3:2** unless the dealer also has Blackjack.
  • Tie (Push): Player gets their bet back.

Basic Strategy

  • Always Split: Aces & 8s.
  • Never Split: 5s or 10s.
  • Double Down: On 10 or 11 if the dealer’s upcard is lower.
  • Hit: If your hand is **12-16** and the dealer shows **7 or higher**.
  • Stand: On **17 or higher** unless it’s a **Soft 17 (A+6)**.

Payouts

Standard Bets

  • **Win:** Pays **1:1**.
  • **Blackjack:** Pays **3:2**.
  • **Insurance Bet (if dealer shows an Ace):** Pays **2:1**.

Side Bets (Trilux Bonus)

  • **Straight Flush:** 9:1
  • **Three of a Kind:** 9:1
  • **Straight:** 9:1
  • **Flush:** 9:1

Blazing 7’s Progressive

  • **One 7:** Pays $10
  • **Two 7’s:** Pays $125
  • **Three Mixed 7’s:** Pays $1,000
  • **Three Suited 7’s:** Pays 100% of the Jackpot

Dealer Tips

  • Always **announce bets, hands, and totals**.
  • Pay **winning hands from right to left**.
  • Ensure **hand signals for hits/stands are clear**.
  • Monitor for **card switching, late bets, or suspicious behavior**.

Game Protection

  • Monitor **big bets and unusual patterns**.
  • Follow **strict shuffle and payout procedures**.
  • Ensure **all cards are dealt properly**.
  • Surveillance **must verify all jackpot payouts**.
