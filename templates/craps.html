Craps Tips and Tricks

Here are some useful tips and tricks for dealing craps.

  • Learn the layout of the table and the odds for each bet.
  • Focus on "Pass Line" and "Come" bets for simplicity.
  • Practice calculating payouts quickly.
  • Pay attention even though players are talking to you.
  • Don't take the game too seriously.
  • Have thick skin.
  • VIG for Buys and Lays: For every $20 placed or won in the lay, there is a $1 charge.

Prop Bet Keys

  • C+E: Craps Hits - 3x bet = 7 to 1, Eleven Hits - 7x bet = 15 to 1
  • Seven-Eleven:
    • 7 Hits: 4x bet = 15 to 1
    • 11 Hits: 7x bet = 15 to 1
    • Aces / Ace-Deuce / Eleven-Twelve: Push (1x)
  • Hi-Lo Split: Hi or Low Hits - 2x bet + 1/2 bet = 30 to 1
  • 3-Way Craps:
    • 12 or Aces Hit - 9x bet = 30 to 1
    • Ace-Deuce Hit - 4x bet + 3/4 bet = 15 to 1
  • Hi-Lo-Yo:
    • Hi or Lo Hits - 9x bet + 1/3 bet = 30 to 1
    • Yo Hits - Push
  • Horn Bet:
    • 11 or Ace-Deuce Hits - 3x bet = 15 to 1
    • 12 or Aces Hits - Push
