Craps - Tips & Guidelines

How to Play

  • Game Type: Craps (played with two dice).
  • Winning Condition: Roll the point number before a 7.
  • Come-Out Roll: 7 or 11 wins; 2, 3, 12 loses; other numbers become the point.

Betting Options & Payouts

Basic Bets

  • **Pass Line & Come Bets:** 1:1
  • **Don’t Pass & Don’t Come:** 1:1
  • **Field Bet:** 2 & 12 pay **2:1**, other numbers pay **1:1**.

Place Bets

  • **6 & 8:** 7:6
  • **5 & 9:** 7:5
  • **4 & 10:** 9:5

Proposition Bets

  • **Any 7:** 4:1
  • **Any Craps (2, 3, 12):** 7:1
  • **Snake Eyes (2) & Boxcars (12):** 30:1
  • **Yo (11) & Ace-Deuce (3):** 15:1

VIG for Buys and Lays

  • **Buy Bets:** Pay true odds but require a **5% commission** upfront.
  • **Lay Bets:** Bet against a number rolling before a 7, paying true odds.
  • **VIG:** For every **$20 placed or won on a lay bet**, there is a **$1 commission**.

Dealer Tips & Procedures

  • Stickperson announces rolls and controls the dice.
  • Base dealers handle bets, payouts, and monitor cheating.
  • Boxperson supervises the table and ensures correct chip handling.

Game Protection & Security

  • Monitor for **late bets or unusual patterns**.
  • Surveillance must verify large payouts.
  • Ensure all **bets follow table limits**.
