Spanish 21 - Tips & Tricks

1. Game Basics

  • Spanish 21 is similar to Blackjack but uses a 48-card deck (no 10s).
  • The dealer hits on soft 16 and stands on all 17s.
  • Players win all ties against the dealer when holding 21.

2. Doubling Down

  • Allowed on any number of cards, even after splitting.
  • Players can "rescue" their double-down bet but forfeit the original bet.
  • Busted double-down hands cannot be rescued.

3. Splitting Rules

  • Players can split any pair up to 4 hands.
  • Aces can be split and hit like any other card.
  • Players can double down after splitting.

4. Blackjacks & Insurance

  • Player Blackjack beats dealer Blackjack and is paid 3 to 2.
  • Insurance is offered if the dealer shows an Ace.
  • Players with Blackjack can take insurance.

5. Surrender Option

  • Players can surrender and get half their bet back (except against dealer Blackjack).
  • Must be done on the first two cards.

6. Bonus 21 Payouts

  • 5-Card 21 → 3 to 2
  • 6-Card 21 → 2 to 1
  • 7+ Card 21 → 3 to 1
  • 6-7-8 (Unsuited) → 3 to 2
  • 6-7-8 (Suited, not Spades) → 2 to 1
  • 6-7-8 (Spades) → 3 to 1
  • 7-7-7 (Unsuited) → 3 to 2
  • 7-7-7 (Suited, not Spades) → 2 to 1
  • 7-7-7 (Spades) → 3 to 1

7. Super Bonus & Envy Bonus

  • If a player's first three cards are suited 7s and the dealer’s up-card is any 7:
  • $1,000 for bets $5-$24
  • $5,000 for bets $25+
  • Other players at the table win $50 each.

8. Match the Dealer Side Bet (MTD)

  • Unsuited match → 3 to 1
  • Suited match → 12 to 1
  • Double match (Unsuited + Unsuited) → 6 to 1
  • Double match (Unsuited + Suited) → 15 to 1
  • Two Suited Matches → 24 to 1

9. Minimum/Maximum Bets

  • Regular max bet: $5,000
  • Match the Dealer min/max: $5 - $200

10. Dealer Bets & Tokes

  • Players can place a toke bet for the dealer.
  • The Super Bonus and Envy Bonus apply to dealer bets if the dealer has the required minimum bet.

11. Common Irregularities & Disputes

  • Face-up card in the deck = Burn the card.
  • Failure to deal a card to a player = That player’s bet is void.
  • If a dealer reveals Blackjack after hitting and standing = All bets lose (except player Blackjacks and doubles).
  • Running out of cards = Shuffle the discard pile and continue.
