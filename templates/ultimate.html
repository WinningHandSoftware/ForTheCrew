Ultimate Texas Hold’em - Tips & Guidelines

How to Play

  • Each player receives **two hole cards** and shares **five community cards**.
  • Players must make **two equal bets** (Ante & Blind Bonus) before the deal.
  • Players can bet **3x/4x, 2x, or 1x their Ante** at different stages of the game.
  • Dealer must have at least a **Pair** to qualify.

Betting Options & Payouts

Blind Bonus Payouts

  • **Royal Flush** - 500:1
  • **Straight Flush** - 50:1
  • **Four-of-a-Kind** - 10:1
  • **Full House** - 3:1
  • **Flush** - 3:2
  • **Straight** - 1:1

Trips Side Bet Payouts

  • **Royal Flush** - 50:1
  • **Straight Flush** - 40:1
  • **Four-of-a-Kind** - 20:1
  • **Full House** - 7:1
  • **Flush** - 6:1

Ultimate Pairs Bonus

  • **A-A vs A-A (Dealer Match)** - 1000:1
  • **A-A** - 30:1
  • **A-K Suited** - 25:1

Dealer Tips & Procedures

  • Ensure all bets are properly placed before dealing.
  • Follow the correct payout order: **Play, Ante, Blind, Trips, Ultimate Pairs Bonus**.
  • Always verify winning hands with a supervisor.
