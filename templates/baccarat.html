Baccarat - Tips & Guidelines

How to Play

  • Game Type: Baccarat (played with 6-8 decks).
  • Betting Options: Banker, Player, Tie, Dragon Bonus, Side Bets.
  • Card Values: Aces = 1, 2-9 = Face Value, 10/J/Q/K = 0.
  • Winning Condition: Closest to **9 points** wins.
  • Natural Win: If first two cards total **8 or 9**, no more cards are drawn.

Third-Card Rule (Simplified)

  • Player: Draws if total is **5 or lower**, stands on **6 or higher**.
  • Banker: Draws depending on Player’s third card.
  • Banker Always Stands: If total is **7** or higher.

Payouts

Standard Bets

  • **Banker** - 1:1 (minus 5% commission).
  • **Player** - 1:1.
  • **Tie** - 8:1.

Dragon Bonus

  • Win by **9 points** - 30:1
  • Win by **8 points** - 10:1
  • Win by **7 points** - 6:1
  • Win by **6 points** - 4:1
  • Win by **5 points** - 2:1
  • Win by **4 points** - 1:1

Side Bets

  • **Dragon 7** - 40:1 (Three-card Banker 7).
  • **Panda 8** - 25:1 (Three-card Player 8).
  • **Fortune 7** - 40:1.

Dealer Tips

  • Announce **all hands and bets clearly**.
  • Collect **losing bets first**, then pay winnings.
  • Follow **third-card rule strictly**.
  • Ensure **all wagers meet table limits**.

Game Protection

  • **Monitor for card switching or late bets**.
  • **Verify large payouts** with surveillance.
  • Ensure players keep **cards on the table**.

Asian Sensitivity & Etiquette

  • Many Asian players **avoid the number 4** (bad luck) but prefer **8** (prosperity).
  • Do **not rush** players – they take their time deciding bets.
  • Be **polite and respectful** – avoid direct eye contact and excessive small talk.
  • Never **touch a player’s lucky chips** or encourage them to color up.
